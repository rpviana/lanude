About Nude

NUDE PROJECT IS A PREMIUM STREETWEAR BRAND FOUNDED IN A SMALL DORM ROOM BY TWO FRIENDS EAGER TO CREATE SOMETHING DIFFERENT.
The brand values transparency in its supply chain and aims to have a positive impact on both the environment and the communities involved in its production.

Nude Project was created by Bruno Casanovas and Alex Benlloch in 2018, while both were still at university. The idea came about in their dorm room, where they began to design and produce their first pieces. Their goal was to create a brand that celebrated authenticity and freedom of expression, quickly winning over a young audience that identified with these values.
